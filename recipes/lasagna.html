Lasagna

Description

On this recipe we aro going to cook a homemade meat lasagna just with a few ingredients, Let's go!

Ingredients

  • Meat: This lasagna recipe contains 1/2 kilo of meat(1/4 kilo ground beef and 1/4 kilo ground pork).
  • Onion adn garlic: We need a diced onion and a garlic cut into small pieces for cook with the meat(don't cry please).
  • Wine: With wine we will give to the meat an extra of flavour.
  • Lasagna sheets: You can buy lasagna sheets oven-ready or do like us and use uncooked sheets.
  • Butter, flour and milk: With the flour and milk we are going to prepare a bechamel.
  • Canned tomatoes We need an can if crushed tomatoes and a can of tomato sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: We are going to use a few seasonings and spices like garlic powder, basil, oregano, salt, back pepper and sugar.
  • Cheese: For this recipe we need al the cheese you can buy!

Steps

  1. First of all we need to mix the ground meet and cook it at medium/high heat on a deep skillet, add the diced onion and garlic, 5 minutes later add a cup of wine and wait until all the alcohol gets boiled.
  2. While the meat is cooking you can start boiling the lasagna sheets and putting it on a clean cloth until it gets cold
  3. For the bechamel we need to cook the butter with the flour and add milk to the mixture, when the bechamel has consistency it's finished.
  4. Now you have to add the lasagna layers starting with the meat, then bechamel and on the top a lasagna sheet to cover the mold.
  5. When the mold is fully covered you have to put a lot of cheese over all the lasagna and cook it for 30 to 40 minutes at 180 degrees C.
  6. Now, the lasagna is cooked, time to turn on the grill and bake for 5 minutes or until cheese get golden brown(remember wait 10 minutes before cutting and serving).
